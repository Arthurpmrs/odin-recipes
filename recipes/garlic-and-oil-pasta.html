Garlic and Oil Pasta

Ingredients

  • 300g of pasta (of your liking)
  • 7 heads of garlic
  • 1 Onion
  • Salt to taste
  • 20 ml of olive oil
  • Seasoning to taste

Steps

  1. Cook up the pasta on a large pan filled up half-full of salted boiling water.
  2. In a frying pan add the olive oil, chopped garlic and chopped onion.
  3. Fry the garlic and Onion until it looks golden.
  4. When the pasta is cooked add it directly to the frying pan.
  5. Add all the seasoning.
