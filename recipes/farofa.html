Farofa

Ingredients

  • 200 g of mandioca flour
  • 60 ml of olive oil or 100g of butter
  • 2 heads of garlic
  • 1/2 of an onion
  • Salt to tast

Steps

  1. Add the olive oil or butter to a pan on medium heat.
  2. Add the onions and shortly after add the garlic.
  3. Add a pinch of salt.
  4. When the garlic and onion are golden, lower the heat and add the mandioca flour.
  5. Add salt.
